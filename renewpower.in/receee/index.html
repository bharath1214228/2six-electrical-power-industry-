About Us

About ReNew Power Centre Of Excellence

Set up by ReNew Power in partnership with IIT Delhi in 2017, the ReNew Power IIT Delhi Centre of Excellence is a state of art research & development facility aimed at developing cutting-edge renewable energy solutions and fostering exchange of ideas between industry, academia and policy makers from across the world. The centre offers research and internship programmes for bright and deserving undergraduate, postgraduate and Ph.D. students. The centre also develops advocacy papers and research reports on renewable energy policy matters for multilateral organizations. One of the key features of the centre is a set of programs to promote women entrepreneurship, startups and other policy initiatives in the field of renewable energy.
